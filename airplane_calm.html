Staying Calm on an Airplane

For first-timers or those who don't fly very often, it can be difficult to stay calm on an airplane. This page provides some tips in order to have a relaxed time while in the air.

Preparing for Your Flight

To make your flight experience as calm as possible, it is important to prepare before your flight. Below is a list of things you can do to make your experience better and calm your nerves.

Breathing Exercises

Slow, deep breathing helps you disengage from your surroundings and unsettling thoughts. Studies have shown that having long exhales relaxes the nervous system, which is responsible for short breaths when panicking. Click on the link for some breathing exercises.

Arrive Early to the Airport

By arriving early to the airport, you add less stress to the whole experience, and you also have more time to get relaxed and calm about the flight.

Bring Distractions

It is easier to avoid the stress of flying if you bring some distractions. Downloaded TV shows or movies on a device, books, or games are great ways to keep you mind on something else. Bring enough so that you are entertained the duration of the flight. Often times, airlines have TVs on the flight with movies playing, and recently, some have a service where you can access their content through your own device. Each airline differs, so be sure to check before deciding what to bring.

Avoid Caffeine

Caffeine increases anxiety and adds nervousness. By avoiding caffeine, you will prevent adding even more anxiety
